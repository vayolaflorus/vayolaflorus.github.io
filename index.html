What I do

I support nonprofit and public-sector organizations in two connected ways. Some teams need one. Many need both.

1. Building staff capacity

Who this is for

Program managers, frontline staff, and leadership teams expected to use digital tools or AI as part of their work.

What this looks like

  • Practical staff training
  • Role-specific workshops
  • Support while teams adapt to new tools
  • Space to slow down and ask questions

Why it matters

When staff feel prepared, decisions feel safer and work feels more manageable.

2. Supporting public-facing programs

Who this is for

Organizations running programs that help community members build digital skills or access technology.

What this looks like

  • Program design and planning
  • Facilitating or supporting participant training
  • Adapting content for different language levels
  • Making sure programs work in real life
