Digital Equity & Technology Training

In my current role with the City of Boston, I work at the intersection of public service delivery, digital equity, and organizational technology adoption. I design and facilitate technology trainings for city staff and residents, support community-based organizations through digital equity initiatives, and help teams adopt new tools in ways that improve access, efficiency, and accountability.

  • Leading digital literacy pilots, facilitating workshops, and providing one-on-one technical support

Previously, I coordinated large-scale adult education and workforce development programs serving immigrant and adult learners across Greater Boston.
