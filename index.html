About

Having worked with nonprofits for over six years, I know firsthand the pressure to adopt AI and new tech when you’re already stretched thin on time, staff, and budget. My research and experience have shown that the stakes are significantly higher when serving vulnerable communities. Integrating AI into these services carries profound ethical weight—requiring a level of care and deliberation that simply cannot be rushed.

I work alongside management teams to make that transition practical and sustainable for their team members. By strengthening internal capacity and supporting public-facing programs, I help organizations ensure both their staff and their community build the real-world digital skills they need to remain resilient and keep pace with a changing world.
